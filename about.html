About BusyBee

BusyBee was created with one mission: to help students find the best study environments wherever they are. Whether you're looking for a quiet library, a cafe with strong Wi-Fi, or a spot with power outlets, BusyBee brings together crowd insights, real-time check-ins, and smart filters to guide your decision.

Inspired by the challenges many students face when looking for reliable study spaces, our goal was to simplify the search by building a tool that’s fast, filterable, and student-friendly. With features like the BusyBee Meter for live crowd levels, location-based recommendations, and customizable filters, BusyBee makes sure you’re always one tap away from your next go-to study spot.
